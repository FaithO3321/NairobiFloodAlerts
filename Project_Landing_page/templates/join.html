Join Us

Nairobi Flood Alerts is a community-driven initiative, and we rely on the active participation of concerned citizens like you. By joining our efforts, you can play a vital role in keeping our city safe and resilient in the face of floods.

Here is how you can get involved:

  • Report flood incidents: Help us stay updated by reporting any flooding situations in your area through our online form or dedicated hotline.
  • Contribute local knowledge: Share your insights and experiences related to flood-prone areas, drainage issues, or community preparedness efforts.
  • Volunteer: Join our network of volunteers and assist with various tasks, such as community outreach, data collection, or distributing emergency supplies during flood events.
  • Spread awareness: Help us raise awareness by sharing our alerts and resources with your family, friends, and neighbors.

By joining Nairobi Flood Alerts, you will receive:

  • Early access to flood alerts and updates
  • Personalized notifications for your area
  • Opportunities to contribute to a safer and more resilient Nairobi

Together, we can make a difference. Join our mission today and be part of the solution.
