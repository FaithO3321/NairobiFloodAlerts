Welcome to Nairobi Flood Alerts, a platform dedicated to keeping you informed and prepared for potential flood events in the city.

We understand the devastating impact that floods can have on communities, and our mission is to provide timely alerts and valuable resources to help you stay safe.

Nairobi is currently under a Safe flood alert level.

Explore our website to access up-to-date information on:

  • Flood Alerts: Stay informed about the current flood situation and potential risks.
  • Regions: Learn about flood-prone areas and the specific estates that may be affected.
  • Rivers: Stay informed about water levels of the rivers within Nairobi and sewer systems that can cause floods.
  • Weather Today: Have real-time information about weather patterns.
  • Be Prepared: Find essential tips and guidance on flood preparedness and safety measures.
  • FAQs: Get answers to common questions related to floods in Nairobi.

Stay vigilant, stay informed, and stay safe with Nairobi Flood Alerts.
